But since you're so inclined to donate money, may I suggest giving it to someone that actually needs it?


The Riley Hospital for Children at Indiana University Health is a children's hospital in Indianapolis, Indiana, United States.

The Foundation is committed to improving the health and well-being of children through philanthropic leadership in support of Riley at IU Health and its research programs. Your gift in any amount makes a difference not only for children who come to Riley at IU Health for world-class treatment, but for families everywhere who benefit from one of the top pediatric research programs in the United States.

Your donation, no matter how small, will fund:

  • Pediatric research
  • Educational opportunities for physicians and medical students
  • Advanced, family-friendly facilities and life-sustaining technology for children
  • Life-changing summer adventures for children with disabilities at Camp Riley
  • Social work, including emergency financial support for families
